Funding and Fees

Crowd Supply follows an all-or-nothing funding model. If your campaign meets its funding goal, then you get all the money raised, less fees. Otherwise, no one is charged and you don't get anything.

Funding

You receive funds at four times:

  • as soon as your campaign hits its funding goal, the pledges up until that point will be processed and you should receive funds within ten days
  • the remaining pledges, assuming your campaign was successful, will be processed as soon as your campaign ends and you should receive funds within ten days
  • once you fulfill all the orders from your original campaign, you will receive funds from the pre-orders placed since campaign
  • whenever Crowd Supply decides to refill its own inventory of your product

Fees

Crowd Supply's fee structure is simple:

  • Credit Card Processing Fee: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
  • Crowdfunding Fee: 5% of total pledged during your campaign
  • Pre-order Fee: optional; 10% of total pledged after your campaign
  • Fulfillment Fee: optional; see Fulfillment and Logistics for more details
